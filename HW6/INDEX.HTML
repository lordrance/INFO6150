Understanding Glaucoma

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, crucial for good vision. This damage is often caused by abnormally high pressure in the eye.

Recognizing Glaucoma

Glaucoma can progress unnoticed until affecting vision. Key signs include blurry vision, halos around lights, intense eye pain, nausea, and redness in the eyes.

Self-Care for Glaucoma

Managing glaucoma involves regular eye pressure checks, adherence to prescribed eye drops, protective eyewear, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
